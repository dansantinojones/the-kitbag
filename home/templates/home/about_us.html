About Us

Our Story

I started collecting retro football shirts back in 2003. I had always been a fond collector, from panini stickers to toy cars but after acquiring a rare Boca Juniors home shirt on my travels in South America my passion for collecting retro football shirts began. Two years later, my garage was full of kitbags stuffed with retro football shirts from all over the world. After some interest on social media, I decided to part ways with some shirts which began the birth of The Kitbag.

What We Do

The Kitbag is an online store selling original and premium retro football shirts. Our aim is to offer the rarest collectables sourced from all over the world and provide the best possible service. Our small team at The Kitbag are all massive football enthusiasts and we pride ourselves on providing the best classic football shirts from the 70’s, 80’s, 90’s and 00’s. We don’t only sell shirts to customers we also buy them. If you have any classic football shirts you would like to sell, please fill in our form on the sell a shirt page and we will take a look. After years of collecting shirts, we now have a strong authentication process to ensure we only buy and sell originals. We do not condone the selling of unauthorised remakes or ‘fakes’.
